Steve Jobs

1955 – 2011 · Co-founder of Apple · Visionary Innovator

About

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and business leader. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc., and played a key role in shaping the modern technology industry through design-driven innovation.

Key Achievements

  • Co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976
  • Revolutionized personal computing with the Macintosh
  • Led the creation of iconic products: iPod, iPhone, iPad
  • Transformed music, phones, and animation industries
  • CEO of Pixar Animation Studios

Legacy

Steve Jobs believed in simplicity, focus, and excellence. His obsession with user experience and design left a permanent impact on how technology is built and used today.
